PC Version (1.9.3)

Bug #1 - "Infinite" durability on tools and armor

In order to do this, you need a pickaxe like for example an golden pickaxe that's been used a bit, and the materials needed to craft it. Go to a crafting table, form the gold pickaxe, and drag it onto you'r damaged pickaxe and boom it should be at full durability and you can keep you'r resources.
